Privacy Policy for tivipal

Last Updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hamid Parsa ("we", "us", or "the Developer") handles information for the tivipal application (App ID: FrXKCd8oHq) on Samsung Tizen OS.

1. Data Collection

As a video player application, tivipal handles the following technical information necessary for app functionality:

  • IP Address: Used in real-time to establish connections to user-provided streaming sources. The Developer does not log or store user IP addresses.
  • Device Unique ID: A non-personal device identifier used solely to manage and verify the free trial period and licensing status of the application. This identifier is stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore (operated by Google LLC) for the purpose of license verification and trial management. It is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

2. Third-Party Services

tivipal uses Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore) as a backend service to store and verify Device Unique IDs for licensing purposes. Data processed by Firebase is subject to Google's privacy practices, available at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

3. Third-Party Content

tivipal is a media player. We do not provide, host, or control the content (M3U playlists or streams) loaded by the user. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external streaming providers, and users should review the privacy policies of any third-party services they connect to.

4. Data Retention and Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent any information to third parties. The Device Unique ID used for licensing is retained for as long as the application remains installed or licensed, and is used exclusively for license verification. Users may request deletion of their Device Unique ID by contacting the Developer.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures, including the security infrastructure provided by Google Firebase, to protect the limited data we handle against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Children's Privacy
