What We Collect

We collect Device IDs and playlist credentials (M3U URLs or Xtream Codes login details) provided by you solely for the purpose of remote synchronisation with your TV device via our cloud service (Firebase Firestore).

How We Use It

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase and is used exclusively to deliver playlist updates to your registered TV device. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any advertising or analytics purposes.

Media Content

tivipal does not host, store, stream, or transmit any media content. All streaming is performed directly between your TV and your IPTV provider's servers.

Payments

Payment processing is handled entirely by PayPal. tivipal does not store any payment card information. We only record whether a device has an active license.

Cookies

We use a single essential cookie to remember your cookie consent preference. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.
